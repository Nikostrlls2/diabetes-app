GlucoPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

GlucoPilot is an education and organization app for people living with type 1 diabetes. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition.

What stays on your device

By default, GlucoPilot stores the information you enter on your own device using local app storage. This can include:

  • saved foods and carb entries
  • glucose notes and reminders
  • account preferences you type into the app
  • whether you enabled AI sharing

When data is sent to OpenRouter

Live AI is off unless you enable AI sharing in the app. When enabled, GlucoPilot may send the food question or coach question you entered, plus a short app summary, to OpenRouter so the AI features can respond.

Do not include names, addresses, phone numbers, insurance details, or other private details in AI prompts.

Exports and deletion

You can export your history from inside the app. You can also delete all local data from the app's Getting Started or Account screen.

Advertising and selling data

GlucoPilot is not designed to sell health data or share it with advertising networks.

Questions or reports

Use the support contact listed on the app store page where you downloaded GlucoPilot if you need help or want to report unsafe or inappropriate AI output.
